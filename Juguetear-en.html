Accesible UI Design for Juguetear

UI Design

About the project

Juguetar is a non-profit organization that has a workshop where electronic toy controls are adapted so that people with complex motor disabilities can use them. The adapted toys are provided free of charge to those who request them.

My role

UX/UI Designer

Duration

2 meses

UI Roadmap

We designed a roadmap to project the interface design of the website.

1.Wireframes design

2.Validation

3.Lo-fi design

4.Developers validation

5.Hi-fi design

6.Stylesheet design

Information architecture and userflow

We define a simple information architecture with few categories. The main objective of the users is to request the loan of the toys. To do this, they must send a form with the data of the child and responsible adult.

Wireframes

The design of the wireframes was very important because it allowed us to validate the accessibility of the final design and the elements that would appear on the website.

Stylesheet

The layout system was also designed and validated following AAA accessibility guidelines.
